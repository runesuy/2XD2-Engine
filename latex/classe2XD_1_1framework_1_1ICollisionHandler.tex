\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Collision handler interface. Handles registration, removal, and collision checking of collision bodies. Calls on\+Collision events on collision bodies when collisions occur.

Can be implemented and injected into the \doxylink{classe2XD_1_1framework_1_1Collisions}{Collisions} system.
